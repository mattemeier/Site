Research Interests

Cognitive Psychology, Working Memory Capacity, Individual Differences, Cognitive Control, Mind Wandering

My research seeks to understand normal individual differences in attentional control by focusing on variation in working memory capacity, the propensity to mind wander and how they may relate to other psychological constructs.
